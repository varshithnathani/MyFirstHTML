Eligibility criteria for ITI

All those students who have completed their Xth can apply for this course and start their fitter engineering. Anyone who wants to apply for this course after their XIIth then must have science and mathematics. Your aggregate percentage should be above 40%. Any candidate that wants to apply for this course should complete their Xth from a recognized state board or CBSE.
